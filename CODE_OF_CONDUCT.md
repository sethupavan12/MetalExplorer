# Code of Conduct

MetalExplorer should be a useful place for people who care about local-first developer tools.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Assume good faith, then verify technical claims with code, logs, or repro steps.
- Keep issue threads focused on the bug, feature, or design decision.
- Make safety concerns concrete.
- Help new contributors understand the project constraints.

## Not accepted

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Sharing exploit details in public issues.
- Repeated low-effort noise after maintainers ask for specifics.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete comments and issues that make the project harder to maintain. Repeated abuse can lead to a ban from the repository.
